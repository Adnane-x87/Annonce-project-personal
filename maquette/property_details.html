Modern Luxury Villa

123 Palm Avenue, Beverly Hills, CA 90210

Description

Welcome to this breathtaking modern architectural masterpiece nestled in the heart of Beverly Hills. This newly constructed estate offers the ultimate California lifestyle with seamless indoor-outdoor living spaces and panoramic city views.

The main level features an open-concept floor plan with soaring ceilings, floor-to-ceiling glass walls, and a gourmet chef's kitchen equipped with top-of-the-line appliances. The living area opens directly to an expansive terrace featuring an infinity-edge pool and spa, perfect for entertaining or relaxing while watching the sunset.

The master suite is a private retreat with dual walk-in closets, a spa-like bathroom with a soaking tub, and a private balcony. Three additional en-suite bedrooms provide ample space for family and guests. Additional amenities include a home theater, wine cellar, smart home automation, and a 3-car garage.

Located just minutes from world-class shopping, dining, and entertainment, this property represents a rare opportunity to own a piece of paradise in one of the most coveted neighborhoods in the world.
